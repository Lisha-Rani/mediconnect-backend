=== CLINICAL TRIAGE GUIDELINES 2024 ===

CONDITION: Sprained Ankle
SYMPTOMS: Sharp pain in the lower leg or foot, swelling, bruising, inability to bear weight.
RECOMMENDED ACTION: Apply ice, elevate the foot, and rest. Take over-the-counter anti-inflammatory medication.
URGENCY: Low

CONDITION: Appendicitis
SYMPTOMS: Sharp pain in the lower right abdomen, gets worse when walking, nausea, mild fever.
RECOMMENDED ACTION: Go to the emergency room immediately for a CT scan and possible surgery.
URGENCY: High

CONDITION: Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack)
SYMPTOMS: Heavy pressure in the center of the chest, pain radiating to the left jaw or arm, shortness of breath, sweating.
RECOMMENDED ACTION: Call 911 immediately. Chew aspirin if available. Do not drive yourself.
URGENCY: Critical
